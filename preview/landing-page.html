How it works

Three steps. Then it runs itself.

01

Write, speak, or paste

Journal entries, voice memos, or imported text. Write as you normally would — you don't need to tag anything or use special formats. The intelligence is in the processing, not the input.

02

AI reads every entry and builds your graph

Each entry passes through several layers: concept extraction, emotional mapping, relationship detection, significance scoring. New nodes appear. Old nodes update. Concepts accumulate relationships over time.

03

Your map gets richer with every entry

The first thirty entries sketch the outline. The next hundred fill in the depth. After a year, the graph has enough data to show you things about your own thinking that you couldn't have articulated yourself.
